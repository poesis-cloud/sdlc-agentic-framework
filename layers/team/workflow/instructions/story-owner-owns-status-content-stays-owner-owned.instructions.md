# Invariant: story_owner_owns_status_content_stays_owner_owned

The Story — its `status:` and its content — stays @product-owner (business) / @system-architect (enabler) owned. @scrum-master governs the status transition through the transition guard (`check-artifact --to`) and the @scrum-master-owned team kanban; it does not write the Story.
